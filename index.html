About

My favorite hobby is reading. I really like to read different stories by diferent authors. I have many books in my room and each one is a special story. I got the habit of reading natuarly. With this love of reading I was able to write a story called " The Trio Adventures A Teachers Secret". I have published this book in Amazon Kindle.

Each and every book has something special about it to me and it will be wonderful to read each one of them. I like to read in peaceful and calm place where there is no sound at all, if I do read in that kind of place I feel like I am with the characters in the book. My favorite authors are Enid Blyton, J.K Rowling, Roald Dhal and many more. And some of the books I like are "The BFG written by Roald Dhal" " The Ship Of Adventure written by Enid Bliyton" and "The Harry Potter Series written by J.K Rowling". I hope you also take up the habit of reading and experience the greatness of it!
